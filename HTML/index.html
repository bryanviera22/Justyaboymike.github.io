Team

Soul-Fighter was founded by Max, Henry, and past members Shahab and Ellie. Shahab and Max trained together in Florida, where Max is from, at the boxing institution “Canino’s Boxing” and then both Max and Shahab came to Pace. Both Shahab and Max missed the workout and spirit of the gym. Thus the idea of Soul-fighter was born.  Though some members have moved on from the club, the spirit of Soul-Fighter remains with the current members who still embody the ideals that both Max and Shahab set at the club's creation.

About Us

Boxing is well known as one of the best full body workouts you can get. The workout is for everybody the same, from full time athletes or absolute beginners. The difference is only the intensity with which someone does the drill. We teach real boxing! Not the fitness version. But sparring with contact is only available outside regular workouts and only for advanced boxers. Through this approach you will learn how to defend yourself simple and effectively, but won´t get hurt in training sessions. The concept was proven and has long been established in the world’s biggest boxing gym: The Pugilist Boxing Gym in Germany. We also have the founder of Pugilist and Aiba 3 star coach Bardia Gharib as technical advisor.
